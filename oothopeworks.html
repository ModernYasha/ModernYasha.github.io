michael ruiz

Michael Ruiz is a freelance Animator and Webcomic Artist who is the creator of “Only On Tuesdays”. He is a multi-talented artist who strongly believes in the beauty and versatility of 2D animation and artistic expression. Michael has dipped his feet in various forms of digital media, including package design, comic illustration, web design, hand-drawn animations, and character design. He has held an art exhibition at the E-Gallery at Rutgers University, where he graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Animation. When Michael is not making art, he has lent his vocal talent to several projects, including Dreamkeepers: The Movie, a fantasy/sci-fi motion adaptation of the popular webcomic of the same name, created by Dave and Liz Lillie and directed by Elson Wong.
